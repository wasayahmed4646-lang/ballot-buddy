Learning Cards

Core election concepts

Eligibility

Most systems require citizenship, a minimum voting age, and registration in the correct area.

Registration

Voters usually submit identity, residence, and age details before a deadline.

Polling Day

Voters verify identity, receive a ballot or use a machine, cast their vote, and leave peacefully.

Results

Officials count votes, resolve disputes through defined rules, and publish certified results.
